I am Nemanja Tomic, a technology professional who enjoys making complex systems more understandable, reliable, and useful.

Most of my day-to-day interests sit somewhere between software engineering, infrastructure, and the habits that help technical teams make better decisions. I like practical tools, clear thinking, and the craft of building systems that age well.

This blog is also shaped by philosophy. I am interested in how we reason about trade-offs, how we work with uncertainty, and how technical choices reflect wider values around responsibility, clarity, and human experience.

If you enjoy writing that sits at the intersection of IT practice and reflective thinking, you are in the right place.
